Events

This page is all about Events and DIYs, where I’ll post fun ideas and interactive stuff you can try at home. One of my favorite things ever is creating things with other people, whether it's drawing, customizing a plush, or just vibing and crafting together. Imagine events like Plushie Design Contests, where people submit their plushie ideas, and the winner gets theirs made (or drawn!). Or maybe we host a cozy online session where we make simple DIY crafts together, like customizing pencil pouches, creating sticker sheets, or freestyle crocheting. These kinds of events are meant to be fun, low-pressure, and full of laughter, even if we mess up; that’s part of the charm. Then there’s the DIY section. I can share DIY ideas like how to make a sock puppet or bedazzle a painted phone case. I want these to be easy, welcoming, and fun for people of all skill levels. Whether you’re a beginner or someone who already crafts every weekend, there’ll be something for you. I believe everyone has creativity inside them, even if they don’t realize it yet. These events and DIYs are my way of helping others discover that spark, because once you start creating things for yourself, even tiny ones, it can make life feel magical.
