Shop

Here in the Shop, I put together all the things I find cozy, useful, or just flat-out adorable. I made sure this space reflects the kind of items that could brighten someone’s desk, sketchbook, or even their entire day. We have three main categories: Custom Plushies, Sanrio-Inspired Plushies, and a whole section for Art Supplies. The custom plushies can be made-to-order based on your OC, your favorite food, or even a silly idea like a flying potato (yes, that one was inspired by a gift I drew for a friend!). Each one is unique and meant to feel personal. Our Sanrio plushies are a huge comfort to me, so I wanted to carry that same vibe, soft, pastel, and full of good energy. As for art supplies, everything here is something I’ve either used before or wish I had when I started. Ibis Paint X was my first app, and now I use Clip Studio Paint, so I’ve tried both digital and traditional tools and picked my favorites to feature here. We even offer beginner sets and mini sketchbook kits to get you going. If you're someone who gets excited about stickers, tiny erasers, washi tape rolls, or even a brand-new brush pen, you’ll probably love it here. I try to imagine what would make someone find what they need. That feeling of joy is what I want to pass on with every item in this shop.
