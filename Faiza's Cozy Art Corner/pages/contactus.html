Contact Us

Need help? Want to ask something? Or maybe you want to say hi? This is the Contact Us page, and I promise it’s not scary. I try to make things easy and human because I get how awkward it can feel to reach out. You can use the little form below to message me about anything, questions about an order, feedback, or even just to chat about art. I love hearing from people, especially if you’ve got a story to share. If you ever ordered something or tried one of the DIYs, I’d honestly love to see how it turned out. If you’re shy about forms, that’s okay too. You can email me directly or reach out on social media. I try to respond within a day or two, unless I’m working on a huge batch of plushies or in full drawing mode. Even then, I’ll always get back to you when I can. I want this corner to feel like you’re talking to a real person, not some cold company. I’m just a girl who loves drawing, cozy things, and building a space for people to feel welcome in. So, whether it’s a quick question or a long message, I’m here, and I’m listening.
