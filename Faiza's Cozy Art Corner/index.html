Welcome

I didn’t come up with the idea of Faiza’s Cozy Art Corner out of nowhere. It started with the feeling I always get when I walk into a space that feels safe, colorful, and full of creativity. I always imagined a place that felt soft, welcoming, and filled with cute things like plushies, art supplies, and warm energy. So, I built this little space to reflect that, even if it’s just online. Before I thought of this fake business of selling plushies and art, I used to just draw for fun. I remember discovering digital art for the first time through YouTube. I was amazed at how beautiful drawings looked on a screen, whether on an iPad, phone, or tablet. That same excitement and inspiration is something I want to bring into this corner. Faiza’s Cozy Art Corner is where you’ll find handpicked items that I genuinely love, like soft brush pens, stickers, washi tape, and custom-made plushies. Some are Sanrio-inspired, because characters like Kuromi and My Melody bring me comfort, too. It took me a while to figure out what I wanted this website to be about, but after thinking about what I love, this is what I created. This website also shares pieces of who I am through my art, events, and DIYs. Whether you're here to look at cozy things, discover new supplies, or just pass the time, I hope the place makes you feel welcomed and matches your aesthetic. Thanks for making this happen.
